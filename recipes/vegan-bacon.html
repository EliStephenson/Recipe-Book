Vegan Bacon

\

Sometimes your stomache just wants a sandwich and there are few sandos on my list that can even be compared to a nice BAT. Before I went vegan I did not particularly enjoy BLT's, whether it was because they were not very healthy or that I tought of bacon as a breakfast exclusive, but after this sandwhich... lemme tell you. I was a changed man. The avacodo, bacon, heirloom tomato, and lemon basil aioliall sing together in harmony and the sauce used for the bacon is so good it's hard not to just drink plain. This is a quick and easy recipe and if you're like me having this with a side of chips is the way to go.

What We Will Need

  • 1/4 C Maple Syrup
  • 1/4 C melted vegan butter
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 1/2 tbsp garlic
  • 1 1/2 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp liquid smoke
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3 ounces extra-firm tofu, pressed
  • 9 sheets of rice paper

Time to Cook!

  1. Whisk together all ingredients except tofu, 1 tbsp of nutritional yeast, and rice paper.
  2. Crumble tofu into small pieces and season with remaining 1 tbsp nutritional yeast and 3 tbsp of the marinade.
  3. Cut rice paper into strips and dip into marinade. Place tofu on top of strips and top with another bacon strip.
  4. Air fry until crispy (or bake at 375 degrees for 12 minutes).
